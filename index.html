About me

I am Md. Musfiqur Rahman (Tuhin), a passionate and creative Graphic & Web Designer with a strong foundation in deep learning and experience leading volunteer initiatives.

My academic work reflects my commitment to leveraging AI for solving real-world problems. I authored a peer-reviewed paper titled A Comparative Analysis of Various Deep Learning Models for Traffic Signs Recognition from the Perspective of Bangladesh, presented at the 2nd International Conference on Big Data, IoT, and Machine Learning (BIM 2023) and published by Springer Nature. Additionally, my research paper Plant Disease Recognition from the Perspective of Bangladesh: A Comparative Study of Deep Learning Models and Ensemble Techniques is awaiting publication in IEEE Xplore and has been accepted for presentation at the 2025 4th International Conference on Electrical, Computer, and Communication Engineering (ECCE 2025)!

With six years of experience in the design industry, I specialize in crafting compelling visuals and intuitive digital experiences. My expertise spans all areas of graphic design, from creating polished print materials and striking illustrations to developing cohesive brand identities. During my internship at the Graphic Design Department of Youth School for Social Entrepreneurs (YSSE), I gained valuable industry insights and honed my skills further. On the web design side, I focus on creating user-centric interfaces that seamlessly blend creativity and functionality, ensuring an exceptional user experience across all devices.

My volunteer journey has been equally fulfilling. I have led and worked with various organizations and initiatives, honing my leadership abilities and making meaningful contributions to professional projects and social causes.

Beyond graphic and web design, I excel in content writing, crafting narratives that captivate audiences and drive engagement.

Let’s connect and explore opportunities to collaborate on exciting projects or initiatives. I’m always eager to take on new challenges and opportunities!
